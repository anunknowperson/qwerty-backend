## Горячие линии Санкт-Петербурга

This section of the website provides a list of hotlines for various services in St. Petersburg:

**Categories:**

* **Закон и порядок (Law and Order):**
    * Police: 02, 102
    * Fire Department: 01, 101
    * Other Emergency Services: See detailed list for phone numbers
* **Социальная защита и обеспечение (Social Security and Support):**
    * Unified Social Number: 8 (812) 334-41-44
    * Social Insurance Fund Hotline: 8 (800) 600-00-00
* **Жилищный фонд и ЖКХ (Housing and Utilities):**
    * See detailed list for specific services and phone numbers
* **Трудоустройство и занятость (Employment):**
    * Employment Center Hotline: 8 (812) 320-06-52
* **Здравоохранение (Healthcare):**
    * Ambulance: 03, 103

* **Транспорт (Transportation):**
    * Traffic Police: 8 (812) 234-90-21
    * Public Transportation Information: 8 (812) 576-55-55
* **COVID-19:**
    * Unified Coronavirus Hotline: 8 (800) 200-01-12

**Details:**

* Each category includes a list of specific services with corresponding phone numbers.
* You can use the "Поиск по названию" (Search by Name) function to find a specific hotline.
* The website also provides information about the "Единые линии по коронавирусу" (Unified Coronavirus Hotlines).



**Other Information:**

* The website features a banner informing users about the use of cookies and Yandex Metrica for website analytics and data collection.
* Users can agree to the data processing policy or close the banner.
